The Greater Margins Advantage

Greater Margins has developed a unique system of investor specific search features that make it faster and easier to find your next investment property.

Value Adder Stars

Greater Margins very own system of property evaluation with investment in mind. Value adder stars show a member property features with potential of higher profits. Square foot for an additional bedroom, higher acreage / double lots, mother in law suites / apartments, garage / outbuildings, central ac / central heat, new repairs, premium finishes.

Profit calculator on each listing

No more switching between websites to estimate your projected profits on your next investment. Greater Margins supplies you with a profit calculator on every listing. If the numbers work for you, easily save the private calculations with the listing in with your favorites.

% Under Market value

Quickly find the listings that are at the lowest % under their after repair value or just feel better about the price you are prepared to pay on your next rental property. Greater Margins makes it simple to see how far below its list price is compared to the properties estimated current value.

Side by Side Comparison

View your favorites side by side and compare everything that makes each one a good deal. Will help you make a better decision when choosing that perfect property to invest in.

Rental return on investment

Easily search by your optimum rental return. Listings include % ROI comparing each properties list price and potential rental profits.
